Turf Fees Explained

Turf fees are the fees that we pay to the clubs we play at for the use of the turf.
They are calculated based on the number of games we play and the number of players in our team.
Every game we play, we owe a certain amount in turf fees, which is decided by the venue.
The more players we have in a game, the less turf fees we owe per player.
However, for each game you play, you owe $10 in turf fees.
Any money left over after we pay the turf fees is used for end of season celebrations and other team expenses.

If you can't afford to pay right now for whatever reason, let me (Erika) know.
